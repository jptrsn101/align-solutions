01 Oil & Gas / Offshore

Aker BP STMF — Data-Driven Reliability Optimisation

USD 2.5M

Frac pumps were inspected and HP iron pressure-tested after every offshore stimulation — adding 10 hours per zone. Wear and failure data, validated by Monte Carlo simulation, confirmed the wear was non-critical. A defined threshold allowed pumps to run through Zone 3 before inspection, shifting from conservative intervention to controlled run-to-limit. A predictive model now forecasts valve life based on sand type, pressure, rate and fluid viscosity.

  • +5 operational days per 28-day campaign
  • USD 500k saved per day
  • USD 2.5M campaign savings

04 Industrial / Reliability Retainer

HPU Reliability Retainer — Hydraulic Pump & Gearbox Investigation

Root Cause

Hydraulic pumps and gearbox on an HPU showed accelerated wear flagged by vibration analysis. Weibull life modelling confirmed wear was system-driven, not ageing. A 2018 overhaul had set all pumps to identical pressure settings, creating vibrational resonance. Introducing a pressure differential resolved it. A design deficiency was also identified: no isolation check valves between pumps and the common discharge manifold.
